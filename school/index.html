Mr. Ram Bahadur Thapa

Principal

सम्माननीय अभिभावक, प्रिय विद्यार्थी तथा विद्यालय परिवार, हाम्रो विद्यालयको आधिकारिक वेबसाइट परीक्षणको क्रममा रहेको कुरा जानकारी गराउँदछु। यो वेबसाइटमार्फत विद्यालयसम्बन्धी सम्पूर्ण जानकारी, गतिविधि तथा महत्वपूर्ण सूचनाहरू तपाईंले प्राप्त गर्न सक्नुहुनेछ। परीक्षणकालका लागि निम्न लिंकमार्फत साइट अवलोकन गर्न सक्नुहुन्छः 🔗 विद्यालय वेबसाइट परीक्षण लिंक gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to Sitganga Secondary School. We are committed to providing quality education that focuses on holistic development of our students. Our dedicated faculty and modern facilities create an environment where students can excel academically while developing essential life skills.

At Sitganga, we believe in nurturing young minds to become responsible citizens and future leaders. Join us in this journey of knowledge and growth.

Mr. Hari Prasad Sharma

Chairman

As the chairman of Sitganga Secondary School, I am proud of our institution's rich legacy of academic excellence spanning over three decades. Our school is more than just a place of learning; it's a community that fosters values, discipline, and character development.

We continually strive to upgrade our infrastructure and teaching methodologies to meet global standards while preserving our cultural values. I invite you to be part of our esteemed institution.
