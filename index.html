About Me

Born in Gulmi Madane 7 Gaaupalika, Nepal.

I grew up in the vibrant city of Bhairahawa, where I completed my schooling and +2 education. I later pursued my bachelor’s degree at Oxford University, where I broadened my horizons academically and personally.

Currently, I work as a Financial Analyst, passionately combining my skills in finance with strategic decision-making to drive success.

My journey from a small village to the global stage has shaped my perspective, and I aim to inspire others through my work and stories.

On this website, you’ll find photos, videos, stories, jokes, news updates, and a special motivation section that combines wisdom from the world’s great holy books and Buddha's teachings.
