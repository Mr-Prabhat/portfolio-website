About Prabhat Panthi

Born in a remote Nepali village, I grew up surrounded by poverty and hardship. Walking miles barefoot to school and working odd jobs to support my family shaped my early years. Yet, these challenges forged an unbreakable spirit within me.

Through relentless determination and a profound mindset shift, I transformed adversity into opportunity. Today, as a global motivational speaker and mindset coach, I’ve inspired thousands with my Unbreakable Mindset Formula, helping them turn struggles into stepping stones for success.

My mission is to empower you to embrace your story, reframe your challenges, and act with purpose. Whether through speaking engagements, coaching, or the Mero Sansaar Tribe, I’m here to guide you toward a life of meaning and impact.

Mission: To empower every individual to transform challenges into opportunities for growth and fulfillment.

Prabhat’s Personal Stories

Real stories of struggle, resilience, and triumph to inspire your journey.

From Poverty to Purpose

Growing up in a remote Nepali village, I knew hunger and hardship as constant companions. My family could barely afford food, and I walked miles barefoot to a makeshift school. At 14, I took on odd jobs—carrying heavy loads, cleaning homes—to support my parents and siblings. Those years were grueling, but they taught me resilience. At 15, I made a vow: my circumstances wouldn’t define me. I studied by candlelight, saved every penny, and sought mentors who believed in me. Slowly, I built a path out of poverty, eventually sharing my story on global stages. Today, I use this journey to inspire others, showing that your struggles are the seeds of your purpose.

The Rise to Global Impact

Building a platform as a motivational speaker was a battle against rejection. Early on, I faced countless “no’s” from event organizers who doubted my story’s power. I juggled jobs, saved for travel, and pitched my vision relentlessly. Each rejection stung, but I saw it as a test. By applying my Unbreakable Mindset Formula, I reframed setbacks as lessons, persisted, and landed my first international speaking gig at 25. That moment changed everything. Today, I’ve spoken to thousands across continents, coaching individuals to transform their lives. This story proves that persistence, paired with purpose, can move mountains.

The Mindset Shift That Changed Everything

At 20, I hit rock bottom. Jobless, broke, and battling self-doubt, I felt my dreams slipping away. One night, staring at the stars in my village, I had an epiphany: my struggles weren’t my enemy—they were my strength. I began journaling my challenges, finding lessons in each one. This shift in perspective—reframing pain as opportunity—became the cornerstone of my Unbreakable Mindset Formula. Within a year, I secured a scholarship, moved to a city, and started building my career. This story is the heart of my coaching, helping others turn their darkest moments into light.

Overcoming Cultural Barriers

As a young Nepali with big dreams, I faced skepticism from my community. “Stay in the village,” they said. “Global stages aren’t for people like us.” But I refused to let cultural expectations limit me. I learned English from old books, practiced public speaking in front of mirrors, and sought out global communities online. Each step was a battle against doubt—both external and internal. By 23, I was speaking at international conferences, proving that your background doesn’t define your destiny. This story inspires my work, encouraging others to break free from limiting norms and chase their dreams.

Building the Mero Sansaar Tribe

After years of speaking, I realized inspiration alone wasn’t enough—people needed community. In 2020, I founded the Mero Sansaar Tribe, a global network for those seeking resilience and purpose. Starting with a small group, I poured my heart into creating a space for sharing stories, challenges, and victories. Facing technical and financial hurdles, I learned digital tools and rallied supporters. Today, the tribe connects thousands, fostering growth and support. This journey taught me that true impact comes from building a community where everyone’s voice matters.

Day 1: Define Your Struggle

Write down one major struggle and how you’ll turn it into strength. Reflect on its lessons.

Day 2: Gratitude Journal

Spend 5 minutes listing three things you’re grateful for to shift your focus.

Day 3: Set an Intention

Choose one clear intention for the day and align your actions with it.

Day 4: Reframe Negativity

Identify a negative thought and rewrite it as a positive opportunity.

Day 5: Take Action

Complete one small action toward a personal goal to build momentum.

Day 6: Spread Kindness

Offer a genuine compliment to someone to foster positivity.

Day 7: Reflect on Success

Write about a past success and what it taught you about resilience.

Day 8: No Complaints

Avoid complaining for 24 hours to cultivate a positive mindset.

Day 9: Visualize Success

Spend 5 minutes visualizing your ideal future in vivid detail.

Day 10: Letter to Future Self

Write a letter to your future self, outlining your goals and hopes.

Day 11: Deep Breathing

Practice 5 minutes of deep breathing to reduce stress and focus.

Day 12: Challenge a Belief

Identify one limiting belief and write why it’s not true.

Day 13: Act of Kindness

Do something kind for a stranger to spread positivity.

Day 14: Celebrate Progress

Review your progress and reward yourself for small wins.

Day 15: Quote Reflection

Choose an inspiring quote from the Quotes tab and journal its meaning.

Day 16: Mindful Walk

Take a 10-minute walk, focusing on your surroundings to clear your mind.

Day 17: Set a New Goal

Create a new goal for the next two weeks and outline steps to achieve it.

Day 18: Practice Forgiveness

Forgive someone who hurt you to release emotional weight.

Day 19: Celebrate Strengths

Write down three things you’re proud of about yourself.

Day 20: Digital Detox

Avoid social media for one day to focus on real-world connections.

Day 21: Set Boundaries

Practice saying “no” to something that doesn’t serve your goals.

Day 22: Create a Vision Board

Build a vision board with images and words representing your goals.

Day 23: Share Inspiration

Share an inspiring story with a friend or the Mero Sansaar Tribe.

Day 24: Gratitude Meditation

Meditate for 5 minutes, focusing on gratitude for your life.

Day 25: Build a Habit

Day 26: Face a Fear

Write down a fear and create a plan to confront it.

Day 27: Connect with Community

Day 28: Reflect on Lessons

Journal about the biggest lesson you’ve learned this month.

Day 29: Plan Bold Action

Day 30: Celebrate Transformation
