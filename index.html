Welcome to My Motivational Space

Hello! I'm Prabhat Panthi, a passionate digital creator born in the serene hills of Gulmi, Nepal. My journey took me from the peaceful landscapes of Gulmi to the bustling city of Bhairahawa, and then to the vibrant town of Murgiya where I grew up.

With a Bachelor's degree completed, I have always been enthusiastic about technology, storytelling, and spreading positivity through creative content. This website is my personal space to share photos, videos, inspiring stories, jokes, and powerful motivational quotes from various holy scriptures.

I believe in continuous learning, evolving, and connecting with people worldwide. Here, you'll not only find glimpses of my personal journey but also content that aims to motivate and entertain.

Feel free to explore and get in touch with me through the Contact section below. Let's stay connected and grow together!

About Me

My Journey

I was born in Gulmi, a beautiful hilly district of Nepal known for its rich culture and heritage. Later, my family moved to Bhairahawa where I experienced a vibrant city life. Eventually, we settled in Murgiya, a place that played a big role in shaping who I am today.

From a young age, I was curious about technology and storytelling. This curiosity led me to pursue a Bachelor's degree, where I honed my skills in digital media and communication.

Besides digital content, I have a keen interest in reading motivational books, listening to spiritual discourses, and understanding various cultures and philosophies.

One of my core beliefs is that every person has a unique story, and through creativity and positive mindset, we can all inspire each other to grow.

Through this platform, I aim to share stories that uplift spirits, jokes that bring smiles, and wisdom from ancient scriptures that enlighten minds. Every piece of content here reflects my journey of learning and sharing.

Outside the digital world, I enjoy traveling, meeting new people, and immersing myself in nature. I believe that staying connected to our roots keeps us humble and grounded.

Thank you for visiting my website. Let's continue this journey together, sharing laughter, wisdom, and positivity across borders!

Motivational Jokes

Why did the motivational speaker bring a ladder to the presentation?

Because he wanted to take his advice to the next level!

Why don't motivational speakers ever get lost?

Because they always find a way!

How many motivational speakers does it take to change a light bulb?

Just one, but the light bulb has to really want to change!

Why did the chicken cross the road?

To prove to the armadillo that it could be done!

What did the grape say when it got stepped on?

Nothing, it just let out a little wine... but then it became vinegar and preserved its usefulness!

Why was the math book sad?

Because it had too many problems... but then it realized every problem was an opportunity to learn!

What's the most positive bone in the body?

The humerus - because it's always trying to be funny!

Why don't eggs tell jokes?

Because they might crack up... but then they become omelets and serve a new purpose!

What did the ocean say to the beach?

Nothing, it just waved... persistently, showing that consistency makes an impact!

Why was the belt arrested?

For holding up a pair of pants... demonstrating that sometimes you have to take one for the team!
