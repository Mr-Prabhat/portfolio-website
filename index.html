About Me

I am Prabhat Panthi, born in the beautiful district of Gulmi, Nepal. My early childhood was spent amidst the hills of Gulmi, where I developed a strong bond with nature and community life.

Later, I moved to Bhairahawa, a bustling city that introduced me to urban culture, new friendships, and countless opportunities. My journey continued to Murgiya, where I balanced my studies with my growing passion for digital content creation.

After years of dedication, I successfully completed my Bachelor's degree, which further fueled my interest in storytelling through visuals and words. My academic journey provided me with knowledge, but my real learning came from life experiences.

Today, I dedicate my time to creating content that entertains, educates, and inspires. Whether it’s capturing a beautiful moment on camera, crafting a compelling story, or sharing a motivational quote, my aim is to spread positivity and spark creativity among my audience.

This website is not just a portfolio but a reflection of my journey, ambitions, and dreams. Let’s connect, share, and grow together!
